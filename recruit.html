Graphic Designer

Warsaw

We are looking for a client-focused Senior Sales Executive with strong experience in custom technology solutions, product strategy and service design. You will be a vital member of a team that provides best in class technology services to our clients - whether it is strategy, digital design, user experience or software development.

Vincit is a premium software development and design agency, best known as Inc. Magazine’s 2019 Best Workplaces and the 2016 Best Workplace in Europe by Great Place to Work Institute. We provide custom software development and design services for well-funded startups and many well-known brands such as Logitech, Yamaha, GE Healthcare, Kellogs’ and more.

The Role

  • Your role is to help companies and organizations make smarter decisions, get better insights and move faster.
  • You possess a clear understanding of agile software development projects from idea to market, outlined in Vincit’s Buyer’s Guide.
  • You are accustomed to working with different levels of your clients' organizations, as well as presenting in front of an executive-level audience.
  • You come with contacts at various companies that could be in need of software solutions for their business.
  • You are not afraid of the business development aspect of your work, and you have the ability to grow your business portfolio outside of current Vincit’s partnerships.
  • You have a solid grasp of how technology works and are comfortable discussing the parameters around custom technical solutions.
  • You align with Vincit’s two KPI’s: Employee Happiness and Client Satisfaction. Growth, profitability and gross sales are byproducts of the two KPI’s implemented well.
  • Your friends describe you as an active, educated and well-spoken achiever with lots of ambition to make an impact, and drive for admirable results.

Here is what we offer:

  • Competitive Base + Incentive Plan
  • Comprehensive health, dental, vision plans available day one.
  • 401(k) plan with 4% match.
  • 10 Days PTO + 10 Federal Holidays.
  • Other
  • Company phone plan, laptop of choice, phone upgrades, monthly massages, company Uber credits, sponsored sports clubs, regular team outings and events, beer on tap and more.
  • Sales-driven and fast-growing organization, with huge pride in the success of our customers.
